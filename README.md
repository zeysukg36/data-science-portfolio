* **`04_EDA_Telco_Churn/`** – End-to-end **Exploratory Data Analysis** on a telecom customer dataset, identifying risk profiles and customer retention strategies.

## 📊 Featured Project: Telco Customer Churn EDA

The latest and most comprehensive project in this repository analyzes customer churn dynamics to help businesses mitigate customer loss.

### 💗 Key Insights & Findings:
* **Contract Risk:** Month-to-month contract customers show a significantly higher churn rate (~42%) compared to two-year contract holders (~3%).
* **Financial Impact:** Churning customers pay \$10–15 more per month on average, indicating a potential price sensitivity issue.
* **Technical Triggers:** Fiber optic users churn at a higher rate than DSL users, signaling potential service or onboarding dissatisfaction.

### 💻 Business Implication:
The riskiest customer profile is characterized by **month-to-month contracts, fiber optic internet, electronic check payment methods, and low tenure**. Implementing targeted retention offers and loyalty discounts for this specific segment can meaningfully reduce churn rates.
